Lasagna

How to make lasagna like an italian

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar(optional)
  • Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan/Pecorinno cheese
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles as is described on the package.
  3. Make the bechamel sauce by stirring milk, nutmeg and a spoon of flour.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Enjoy your meal with family, friend or some good italian wine!
