Crockpot Italian Chicken

How to make Italian crockpot chicken like an italian

Making Italian crockpot chicken is a fast and tasty recipe. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions below.

Ingredients

  • Italian dressing
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Stir Italian Sauce and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in oven preheated at 180 Celsius, and pour dressing mixture over chicken. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.
  3. Enjoy your meal with family, friend or some good italian wine!
